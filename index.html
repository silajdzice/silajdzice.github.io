About me

My name is Edhem Silajdzic, I was born in Sarajevo in September 1992 and I am mobile engineer with strong knowledge about frontend and great visual solutions. I have always had a strong drive to do IT, even though I have studied economy, programming was huge part of my life, so I have attended IT academy, and got an internship immediately after finishing academy. Through my internship and work experience, I had a chance to adopt knowledge and work as QA engineer, later on to work with frontend full-stack technologies like meteor.js and react.js. That gave me a deep understanding of product development process. I found my passion and interest in developing iOS applications and games, and that is where my career goes on.
